About Me!

Total Species Photographed So Far: 124

My interest in birds only truly started in 2020 but once it started, it took off. I decided to express my love of birds in the form of photography. I've been photographing birds for a little under a year, and it's allowed me to see many different behaviors that I never would have noticed before. My favorite birds (at the moment) are Harris's Hawks and Vermillion Flycatchers.
